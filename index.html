09/26/20. Why not social media/make-a-website for this?

I completely forgot to mention in the video and in here why I chose not to make something like twitter or facebook, whatever social media. It's because I don't like social media. The idea of it is shallow, so it's not worth it. I won't make one even at the cost of not being able to interact with people. Besides, if you want to contact me, you know where to do so.

Also, I can do fancy stuff and completely personalise it. Yeah, I could go onto those websites like wix or wordpress, but then the domain would contain their respective url and that sucks. What also sucks is that you don't have complete control over it as well (wix forces ad in footer or something.) What also also sucks is that there'll be no sense of creation if I'm not making it from scratch. Using a template and stuff is lame, even if it's easier.

All in all, I prefer to have control over my stuff. With github, at least I get to do anything I want (except domain name 'cuz that requires purchasing or somehow finding a free one which I can't be bothered to do.)

Sep 26, 20. Rejected Projects 1 - Impostor Sydrome

A week or so ago, I made a short comedic story about imposter syndrome. The plan was to write it, edit, and then publish as most projects go... this didn't happen for one main reason. I think the humor was too dark in this one. It had a sort of obvious punchline and the payoff wasn't really funny at the end so I decided, "yeah. this one is pretty stupid." And decided to drop it.

I'll just give a summary of how the story went.
> Person suffers from severe impostor syndrome. No confidence, low self-esteeem, comparing others and feeling left behind.
> Tasks person does seem to amount to nothing. Having trouble working because of internal violent conflict. > Other people believe in person's efforts but person feels guilty.
> Wake up to another loud noise.
> Can't vent the sentiments, must make everyone think person is fine, even though person is fake.
> Kill people
> Kill increases confidence, higher self-esteem, no more comparing if no one to compare. Syndrome is pushed by constructs of society, therefore killing society means no more syndrome equal happy.
> Person is An Impostor.
> it was an Among Us joke.

What's the lesson to take away from this? Do your work.
